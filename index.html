The Era of Absurdity is Over.

In an era where AI writes code in seconds, we still spend evenings resetting passwords for utility portals and manually typing 26-digit bank accounts.

ConciergePay is a manifesto for reclaimed time. An invisible layer between you and corporate bureaucracy.
